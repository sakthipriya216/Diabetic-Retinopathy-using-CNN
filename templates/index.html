Understanding Diabetic Retinopathy

Diabetic retinopathy is a complication of diabetes, caused by high blood sugar levels damaging the back of the eye (retina). It can cause blindness if left undiagnosed and untreated. The manual diagnosis process of DR is time and cost consuming process, it's also prone to misdiagnosis. So we have used a deep learning approach to detect Diabetic Retinopathy. We aim to make DR assessments faster and more precise, greatly lowering the chance of errors. This helps in detecting the disease early and can significantly reduce the risk of vision loss.
